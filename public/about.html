About Us

Whenever people are commuting to go to work or school, most of the time they face an inconvenience when they are trying to pay for their parking. People must wait for the payment to be processed for their parking spot and that could waste time for them to get on the train, go to class, and/or get to work on time. In order to satisfy the commuters daily, EZ2Park is in the process of constructing a parking system that uses license plate recognition, which is called QuickPark.

This system would be able to recognize the user via license plate of their vehicle when they are entering and exiting the parking lot. Users will be able to get paid automatically once they get out of the parking lot because when they entered, the system recognized the car and recorded what time they entered the parking lot. Once they reach the exit, the system will record the time when they exited the parking lot and will calculate the total for the parking between the two times they entered and exit. Based on the survey responses, we developed the marketing and engineering requirements, design alternatives and preliminary design.
